Personality

The maltese is a loyal dog, fearless, intelligent, playful, easygoing, (like myself of course. It is also very responsive, so it would be an fairly easy canine to train. Besides it a cute dog. It is very sweet tempered to add to the mix.

All about the fur

The fur is as it looks, it is very soft and not to mention a major plus. The fur rarely sheds unlike other dogs. Which makes it a great indoor dog!
